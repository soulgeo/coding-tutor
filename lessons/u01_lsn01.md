## Welcome to the Terminal!

Welcome to your first step in becoming a programmer! Let's learn how to use your new workspace.

The box on the right is your **Code Editor**. This is where you write your Python instructions. The box below the editor is the **Console** (or Terminal). When you tell Python to "print" something, the result appears here. It’s like the screen of a calculator.

---

### Your First Task
Look at the code in the editor. It currently says `print("I am a programmer!")`.

1. Press the **Run** button to see "I am a programmer!" appear in the console.
2. Press the **Submit** button to run and submit your code and finish the lesson.

## True or False?

In programming, some things are simple: they are either **True** or **False**. These are called **Booleans**.

Notice that in Python, `True` and `False` must start with a **Capital Letter** and they don't have quotes. If you put quotes around them, they become strings!

Booleans are the foundation of how computers make decisions.

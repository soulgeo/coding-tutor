## Unit 4 Recap

Lists allow you to handle large amounts of data. The most common pattern is looping through a list and using an `if` statement to filter or transform the data.

---

### Your Final Unit 4 Challenge: Sale Scanner
You are building a scanner for a store. If a price is too low, it gets a "DISCOUNT" label. If it's high, we apply a 2x luxury tax and show the new price.

1. Write a `for` loop that iterates through the `prices` list.
2. Inside the loop, use an `if/else` statement:
   - If the price is **5 or more**, calculate the price times 2 and `print` the result.
   - Otherwise (if the price is less than 5), `print` the string `"DISCOUNT"`.
3. Click **Submit**.

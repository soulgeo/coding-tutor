## Numbers & Math

Python is an amazing calculator. It can handle simple math and complex equations instantly.

### Basic Operators
You can use these symbols to do math:
- `+` (Addition)
- `-` (Subtraction)
- `*` (Multiplication)
- `/` (Division)

Notice that you **don't** need quotes for numbers. If you put quotes around a number, like `"5"`, Python treats it as text (a String) and can't do math with it!

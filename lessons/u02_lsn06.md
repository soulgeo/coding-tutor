## Combining Logic

Often, one condition isn't enough. We can use `and` and `or` to create "Security Clearances" in our code.

- `and`: Both conditions must be `True`.
- `or`: At least one condition must be `True`.

```python
if is_weekend or is_holiday:
    print("Time to relax!")
```
